Limited Staples

This project aims to illustrate staples in the traditional draft format (BO3) of Magic: The Gathering. The staples are learned from trophy decks from 17lands recent 500 trophy decks. This site has the following features:
  • For each color pair, list a template deck consisting of 2 rares, 5 uncommons, and 13 commons
  • For each color pair, name the top 3 rare, top 7 uncommon, and top 10 common cards
  • For each color pair, show the cards worth splashing
  • Lastly and most importantly, search a card by name to return its usages in decks

Do one thing; Do it well.
